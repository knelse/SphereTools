100 Before you is a scroll with a recipe of a magic potion inscribed upon it. Behind every such recipe lies a life's work of several generations of mages. Make sure to save such a valuable possesion in your special book of recipes.

TEXT

    To compose a powder of magical defense, first obtain some spherite and mandragora. Mix those in an alchemist's pot, add Fainal's powder and enjoy the resulting powder. Do not forget that to perform this reaction your title should be no less than a candidate.
