TEXT

    In order to obtain the poisonous flame powder, first of all find Eritrite, Florus and Iron. Mix them in the alchemical pot, add the Amilus powder to complete the reaction and use the obtained powder. Don’t forget that in order to make that reaction your degree should be no less than that of a pupil.
