03 On the merchants

100 In front of you is a scroll with an ancient legend from the life of the Sphere.

TEXT

    Many a merchant and artisan sell their goods far away from bustling towns: at remote inns or in their own houses situated close to larger roads. A strange practice, you would say, to sell something far from towns, but it is the high quality of goods that make those merchants’ shops crowded with customers.
