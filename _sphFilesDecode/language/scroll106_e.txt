TEXT

    In order to get the weakness powder you will have first of all to find the Flint, Melvera and Iron. Mix them in the alchemical pot, add the Amilus powder to make the reaction complete and use the obtained powder. Don’t forget that in order to make that reaction, your degree should be no less than that of a pupil.
