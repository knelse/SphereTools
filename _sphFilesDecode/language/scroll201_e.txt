03 On gigantic monsters 

100 In front of you is a scroll with an ancient legend from the life of the Sphere. 

TEXT

    Sometimes as a results of mutations some downright fearful monsters appear to the light: gigantic, ferocious and dangerous. Many a valiant knight lost their heads trying to oppose those limbs of the devil. There are rumors that one of such monsters is dwelling in an old crater to the south-west of Sheepstone.
