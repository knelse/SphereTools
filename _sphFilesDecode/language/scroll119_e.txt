100 In front of you is the scroll with the description of the magic powder. Behind each formula there is a painstaking and time consuming effort of several generations of magicians. In order not to lose such a valuable scroll, it’s better to save it in the special formula book.

TEXT

    In order to obtain the lightning powder, first of all find Pirite, Serden and Iron. Mix them in the alchemical pot, add the Amilus powder to complete the reaction and use the obtained powder. Don’t forget that in order to make that reaction your degree should be no less than that of an novice.
